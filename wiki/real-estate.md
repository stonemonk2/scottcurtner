### Scott's Tenant Transition Playbook

Scott's standard approach when a long-term tenant vacates:

1. **Rebate offer** — offer outgoing tenant $200 rent rebate in exchange for showing the unit to prospective tenants and providing candid feedback on candidate vibes. Builds goodwill, keeps vacancy coordination simple.
2. **Deposit return** — aim to return full deposit promptly and cleanly. Sets the tone for a good reference relationship.
3. **Walkthrough** — schedule before move-out to assess condition and scope any needed work.
4. **Above-market placeholder ad** — post an intentionally high-priced ad with older photos during renovation period. Serves as pricing anchor for other tenants and buys time for upgrades.
5. **Post-reno repricing** — assess true market after upgrades and update the ad with real price and fresh photos.

Scott does not always price vacancies at true market rate immediately. Above-market placeholder ads serve multiple purposes: anchoring existing tenants, buying renovation time, and testing the market ceiling before committing to a price.
